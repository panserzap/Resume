About Me

I’m a Senior Software Developer based in Manchester, with 5+ years of experience across C++, Python, Unreal Engine, automation tooling, and application development.

My background combines industry software development with applied research. At REALTIME UK, I build backend Unreal Engine plugins, automation pipelines, internal tools, and data-driven configurator systems for real-time 3D automotive and yacht projects. I’ve also worked on machine-learning-based visual quality control for rendered images, helping reduce manual review work and improve delivery workflows.

Previously, I worked as a Research Associate at CERTH, where I led a Work Package on the CoRoSect European Horizon 2020 project and developed AR/VR, robotics, real-time object detection, and point-cloud processing applications.

I enjoy building practical software that makes workflows faster, clearer, and more reliable. I’m particularly interested in roles involving Python, C++, tools development, automation, backend systems, data-driven applications, real-time 3D, simulation, and visualisation.
