About Me

I am a well-rounded software developer with a diverse background spanning both industry and research. I began my career as a Research Associate in Greece, where I worked on innovative projects using Python, C# with Unity, machine learning (ML) and deep learning (DL), robotics (ROS), and immersive AR/VR development with HoloLens (using SenseGlove) and HTC VIVE.

Later, I joined REALTIME—a BAFTA-nominated VFX studio in the UK—initially as a junior programmer and later as software developer. There, I work on cinematic and interactive applications using Unreal Engine, C++ & Blueprints, and Python; I also employ web technologies (HTML, CSS, PHP, Flutter) and evaluate ML/DL models for anomaly detection. My work includes developing plugins to streamline artist workflows and collaborating on car configuration projects for renowned automotive clients.

I am actively seeking opportunities that allow me to leverage my expertise in C++, Python, and Unreal Engine to drive innovation and create cutting-edge solutions.
